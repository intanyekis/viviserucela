    The All Elite Wrestling star showed up at the site of WWE RAW last night in Chicago and spoke with some talents backstage, including Triple H, before being asked to leave. So how did all of this come to happen in the first place?

    In an extensive update from Mike Johnson of PWInsider, CM Punk was traveling back from Flordia, where he was doing commentary for Cage Fury Fighting Championships. The All Elite Wrestling star ended up on the same plane as several WWE talents and was informed during the flight that RAW was scheduled for the Allstate Arena in his hometown.

    Punk decided to go at some point, and some WWE talents got him into the backstage area. He remained in what was described as the “common area,” and it was there he interacted with both The Miz, who he reportedly squashed past heat with, and Triple H, who told him he needed to check in with the “big guy” to make sure it was okay that he was there.

    Roughly 20 minutes after arriving, WWE Security approached Punk and informed him that he wouldn’t be allowed to remain there. The All Elite Wrestling star was respectful of the decision, said his goodbyes, and left without an issue.
