    Nick Cannon's baby mama Bre Tiesi shares what plastic surgery she's had done

    Bre Tiesi is down to do anything to keep her youthful appearance. During an Instagram Q&amp;A session with fans on Instagram Monday, the Selling Sunset newbie and one of Nick Cannons baby mamas got candid about the work she has gotten so far.

    24 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Bre Tiesi is “down to do anything” to keep her youthful appearance.

    During an Instagram Q&A session with fans on Instagram Monday, the “Selling Sunset” newbie and one of Nick Cannon’s baby mamas got candid about the work she has gotten so far.

    “I have had my nose done, I’ve had my boobs done, I’ve done filler, I have done Botox, I’ve tried literally everything,” she disclosed. “Kybella, Morpheus, like, I just did a CO2 laser. I’ve done a ton of lasers.”

    Tiesi, 32, added that she and her best friend will be “the guinea pigs for anything you can do to fossilize and keep this forever.”

    The real estate agent also spoke about her cosmetic work during her debut in Season 6 of “Selling Sunset,” which hit Netflix on May 19.

    While showing a multimillion-dollar home to rapper Saweetie, Tiesi told the “Best Friend” musician that the “only thing fake in the house” was the faux grass in the garden.

    “Besides maybe my hair,” the mom of one added, “and my boobs … and my nose.”

    Aside from being open about her plastic surgery, Tiesi has also been vocal about her unconventional relationship with Cannon, with whom she shares 10-month-old son Legendary.

    On several episodes, the reality star had to defend her “open relationship” with the “Masked Singer” host — who is dad to a total of 12 kids with six women — to her co-star Chelsea Lazkani.

    “I find Nick Cannon and Bre’s relationship rather off-putting,” the British-Nigerian real estate agent said on an episode. “Ultimately, the way I live my life is very different to her as a Christian, so I don’t know if we will ever be super, super close friends.”

    Lazkani also refers to Cannon, 42, as a “master manipulator” and claims “creating multiple broken homes is disgusting.”

    Once Tiesi got wind of her fellow Oppenheim Group colleague’s comments, she confronted her during a work event.

    “Who I have children with is my business,” she said to Lazkani. “I don’t need a judge or jury.” 

    Season 6 of “Selling Sunset” is available to stream on Netflix.
