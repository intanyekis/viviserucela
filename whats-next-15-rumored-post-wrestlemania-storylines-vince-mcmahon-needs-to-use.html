    The aftermath of WrestleMania 36 will see wrestlers having new storylines and plans on WWE television. Despite the empty arena landscape, the shows are completely different, but WWE is set on continuing to make it work to provide some form of new entertainment. WrestleMania was a huge event with WWE showing new presentations and having some new title changes.

    RELATED: 7 Wrestlers That Weren’t Allowed To Attend WrestleMania 36 (And 7 Vince Had Nothing For)

    Quite a few fascinating rumors are circulating about the future of WWE. New feuds, character changes and wrestlers appearing on new shows are expected. WWE will try to find a groove, so they are in a healthy place when things get back to normal. Find out what to look forward to over the next few months with some interesting rumors about WWE after WrestleMania.

    15 Big Show becoming next victim to Firefly Funhouse

    Big Show made a surprise return challenging Drew McIntyre for the WWE Championship saw him back in the WWE world. However, another main eventer Big Show wants to work with is Bray Wyatt. The outstanding Firefly Funhouse spectacle with John Cena saw Big Show wanting to have a similar match facing his career of fears.

    RELATED: 15 Little Known Facts About The Fiend’s Life Outside Of The Ring

    14 Bianca Belair having undefeated streak on main roster

    The debut of Bianca Belair on the main roster for WrestleMania 36 sees her working with the Street Profits moving forward. Belair is one of the most athletic workers in the company with a world of potential. There are rumblings that Belair will have a long undefeated streak before finally getting a title shot.

    13 Drew Gulak turning on Daniel Bryan

    The duo of Daniel Bryan and Drew Gulak have been feuding with Sami Zayn, Cesaro and Shinsuke Nakamura in recent weeks. It wasn’t too long ago that Gulak and Bryan were feuding with each other before finding common ground. Gulak turning on Bryan would make the most sense as Zayn’s group continues to grow.

    12 Bobby Lashley and Lana splitting up

    Lana’s guidance for Bobby Lashley cost him a match at WrestleMania 36 against Aleister Black. Lashley’s post-match interview backstage saw him implying it may be time to divorce Lana as a partner and manager. Both wrestlers would better off moving on after their lackluster time together in a few horrible storylines.

    11 Dominant tag team run for Cedric Alexander and Ricochet

    Ricochet and Cedric Alexander both struggled to breakout over the past few months in singles action. The two friends started teaming on Raw and already look like a great duo. Ricochet and Alexander each have unlimited potential. The plans to have them become a long-term team will see them achieving success if WWE commits to the push.

    10 Becky's dominance not ending

    Becky Lynch shocked the world by having another successful defense over Shayna Baszler at WrestleMania 36. The Raw Women’s Championship reign of Lynch has lasted for over a full year now with no end in sight. Lynch will likely not drop the belt until someone else proves they are ready to gain such a win.

    9 Gronk having match while quarantined for 24/7 Championship

    Rob Gronkowski left WrestleMania 36 as the 24/7 Champion after pinning his best friend Mojo Rawley. WWE did this with the plan of having a future segment set up for the former NFL star to eventually lose it. Gronk losing it at home to an invading Mojo while quarantined is a funny rumor that would make sense and provide entertainment.

    8 Bayley continuing to use Sasha Banks until split

    The shift in the relationship between Bayley and Sasha Banks sees Bayley as the one exploiting her best friend after it was always the other way around. Bayley has manipulated Banks into sacrificing to continue the Smackdown Women’s Championship for her. This is expected to continue until Banks finally ends the friendship to get their singles feud going.

    RELATED: 14 Photos Of Bayley Without Her Usual Wrestling Gear

    7 Mustafa Ali as truth-teller

    A mysterious figure revealed the truth behind Dolph Ziggler and Sonya Deville sabotaging the budding romance between Mandy Rose and Otis. Mustafa Ali is the rumored wrestler behind this gimmick that brings light to the wrongs going on in WWE today. The gimmick would help Ali get over and have multiple storylines moving forward.

    6 Sonya Deville getting better of Mandy Rose

    Sonya Deville and Mandy Rose are no longer friends after Sonya manipulated Mandy on a few occasions. The belief is that Deville will have a stronger push since WWE wants a few heels to step up. Rumors of Deville being built up to eventually move to Raw to challenge Becky Lynch will need a few major wins over Rose and others.

    5 Edge feuding with AJ Styles

    AJ Styles was buried by The Undertaker at the end of their Boneyard match. WWE will need to write a way to bring Styles back, but his performance ensured he’ll have momentum when returning. Edge named Styles as one of the top wrestlers he most wanted to face when coming out of retirement. WWE will work to make it happen by the summer or fall at the least.

    4 Asuka feuding with Kairi Sane

    The rumors of Kairi Sane’s contract coming to an end soon may see her wanting to move back to Japan. Sane was doing well in a tag team with Asuka, but it wasn’t a guarantee by any means. Asuka has started wrestling more singles matches in recent weeks. If Sane is on the way out, Asuka will likely have a feud with her partner to benefit from the departure.

    3 Seth Rollins becoming first title feud for Drew McIntyre

    Reports have been going around for months that WWE wanted to have a WWE Championship program between Drew McIntyre and Seth Rollins. McIntyre winning the title sees him in need of facing new heels. Rollins has been one of the top villains in WWE and would create great momentum for the title run.

    2 Bray Wyatt ending Braun's title reign shortly

    Bray Wyatt losing the Universal Championship to Goldberg looks like a disaster based on everything to happen with the changes in the booking. Braun Strowman defeated Goldberg as a last-minute change. Wyatt is already entering a feud with Strowman with the likelihood of winning the title back since WWE didn’t plan for Braun to win it until backed into a corner.

    1 John Cena coming back as broken heel

    John Cena disappearing at the end of the Firefly Funhouse had fans speculating about his future. The match saw Cena placed into various scenarios of his fears coming to life before “letting Bray Wyatt in.” Cena will eventually return, but this may be the ideal explanation for a heel turn to finally happen.
