    27 Jul 2024, 00:00

    FORT MILL, S.C. — The husband-and-wife team behind Flipside Café are expanding their portfolio with a new restaurant in Fort Mill.

    Chefs Amy and Jon Fortes plan to open FM Eatery — also known as the Fortes Mill Eatery — in December at 217 Main St.

    That space was formerly home to Local Dish. The family-owned- and-operated restaurant closed in May after a decade.

    The Forteses have leased that building.

    “We’ve always wanted a space in downtown Fort Mill,” Jon Fortes says. “We just wanted to be part of that Main Street vibe.”

    Plans call for a 54-seat restaurant with an upscale bar.
