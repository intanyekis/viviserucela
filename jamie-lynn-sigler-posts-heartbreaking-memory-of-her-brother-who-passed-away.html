    25 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Earlier today, actress Jamie-Lynn Sigler posted a heartbreaking and beautiful tribute to her brother in memory of the sixth year of his passing.

    New York stockbroker Adam Sigler died at the tender age of 41 back in 2014 after having suffered a brain hemorrhage. Adam slipped into a coma shortly thereafter and sadly passed away surrounded by his family and loved ones.

    RELATED: Jamie-Lynn Sigler: All The Ups And Downs In Her Life

    Best known for her role as the sassy Meadow Soprano on the hit mob show TheSopranos, Sigler rose to fame at a young age. Only a year prior to the death of her brother, Sigler had suffered the traumatic and sudden loss of her on-screen father, the incredible actor James Gandolfini (known for his role as Tony Soprano) who passed away in 2013 at the age of 51 after suffering a heart attack.

    TheSopranos actress took to her Instagram today to share a family photo of the Sigler family, as well as a solo photo of her and her brother Adam captioned, "Our last family dinner, and the only red carpet I ever took you on because I was afraid you were going to hit on every female at the event..6 years since my bro is gone, but man you are doing some big things for all of us up there. Miss you every day".

    At age 20, Sigler was disanosed with Multiple Sclerosis (MS), a potentially disabling disease.
