    Jessica Actress

    Jessica started her acting career with theater plays. Her first television role was on The District in 2004 and her first film role was in Neo Ned in 2005. Jessica gained prominence with her appearance in Seth MacFarlane’s 2012 film Ted as Tami-Lynn, the title character’s love interest. She repeated the role in Ted 2 (2015).

    In 2012, Jessica accused her manager David Guillod of drugging and sexually assaulting her. She re-stated the accusation in 2017. Guillod was charged in June 2020 with 11 felony counts of sex-related crimes, such as kidnapping and rape, stemming from accusations made by other women.

    Jessica and Harvey Weinstein

    Jessica disclosed in 2017 that she was a victim of sexual harassment from Harvey Weinstein. They met in Harvey’s hotel suite for a presumed business meeting where Harvey alternated between offering to cast her in a film and demanding a naked massage in bed. MacFarlane, who directed Jessica in both Ted movies, stated his support for her allegations in a statement on Twitter
