    He’s crafted a style of music for himself which has been denoted as a fusion of grime-y Hip Hop, progressive rap and spoken words.

    Being a poet, he gets his inspiration for art and music from his surroundings and its happenings. This has a large influence on his songs lyrics.

    His poetic style of music therefore made him unique and helped him gain recognition from top entities.

    Kojey Radical developed the passion for music and art at a very young age and then buttressed his knowledge by studying in London College of Fashion where he attained a B.A hons degree in fashion illustration.
