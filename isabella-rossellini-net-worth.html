    Born on June 18, 1952 in Rome, Italy, Isabelle Rossellini is an Italian actress, author, filmmaker, model and philanthropist. Her net worth has been estimated to be an impressive $65 million.

    Isabelle had a long running modeling campaign with Lancome where she was associated for 14 years. She has also acted in many movies. She is known for her roles in movies such as Death Becomes Her and Blue Velvet, where she played the role of a tortured night club singer Dorothy Vallens. She did her own signing for this movie as well.

    She made her film debut as a nun opposite her mother in the film A Matter of Time in 1976. She then worked in the film Il Prato. She played the role of Gabriella Valentino in Silent Life which was released in 2012.

    Rossellini wrote and co-directed a series of short films called Green Porno in 2008. Each film in Green Porno is for two minutes. She was announced as the President of the Jury for the 61st Berlin International Film Festival in 2011.

    She has dual Italian and United States citizenship. Rossellini was married to Scorsese from 1979 to 1982. After her marriage ended, she married Jon Wiedemann in 1983 and divorced him in 1986.
