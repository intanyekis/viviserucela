    Wendy Bilas Painter

    Bilas is a vastly recognized painter who currently serves as a contemporary impressionist painter. It is known that her paintings focus on the effects of light. Moreover, she renders them with loose and expressive brushwork.

    Citing her work, Wendy transforms familiar vistas and images into bold interpretations of color, rhythm, and emotion. Most noteworthy, she uses her own sense of composition as she draws her inspiration from classic impressionists.

    Furthermore, Bilas’ calculated brushstroke gives her work a simplicity and a soulfulness all its own. As a result, she was recognized as an Art with Heart Emerging Artist in North Carolina. Likewise, she has been honored in juried shows throughout the Southeast.

    On the other hand, Wendy works out of her studio in Charlotte, NC. Her work is available at Sozo Gallery and at charity art auctions and events across the country. She has been painting professionally since 2002.
