    Overall Meaning

    The song "Lost In Emotion" by Lisa Lisa & Cult Jam is about a woman who is very much in love with a man who may or may not reciprocate her feelings. She feels lost in her emotions and unable to control her feelings. The song is addressed to this man, with her asking him about his true feelings for her. She is telling him things that she feels she shouldn't be sharing, and she wonders if she is a fool for being so open with him. She wants to believe the rumors that he has a crush on her, but she is also afraid of being hurt.


    The lyrics are very raw and emotional, with the singer expressing her vulnerability and insecurity. She is putting herself out there, risking rejection and heartbreak, but she can't help herself. The chorus repeats the phrase "lost in emotion," which captures the feeling of being overwhelmed by strong feelings of love and attraction.


    Overall, the song speaks to the universal experience of falling in love and feeling unsure of whether or not the other person feels the same way. It's a poignant and heartfelt ballad that showcases Lisa Lisa & Cult Jam's talent for soulful, emotional music.
