    On Tuesday, Visual Concepts published a new patch for WWE 2K23. Patch 1.15 for WWE 2K23 is now available. The contents of this patch have yet to be revealed, however it is anticipated to contain bug fixes. According to what is reported on the internet, the patch is approximately 1.7 GB in size. When the most up-to-date WWE 2K23 patch notes are available at wwe.2k.com, we will update this article.

    The Hot Topic 

    Patches for video games have recently been a major topic of debate. Kenny Omega told fans ahead of the debut of AEW Fight Forever that developers will continue to maintain the game. ‘Hot Fix 1.11’ was released earlier this month, addressing failures and other problems with stability.

    Bray Wyatt’s new entry 

    Bray Wyatt’s WWE 2K23 entrance was made public earlier this month, ahead of the corresponding DLC pack’s release the following week. In addition to Wyatt, the ‘Revel with Wyatt’ DLC pack will include Uncle Howdy, Blair Davenport, Joe Gacy, Valhalla, and Zeus. WWE 2K revealed Wyatt’s entrance during a livestream promoting the DLC pack and the game’s forthcoming update, which you can see in the video below. The Revel with Wyatt DLC pack will be available on July 19.
