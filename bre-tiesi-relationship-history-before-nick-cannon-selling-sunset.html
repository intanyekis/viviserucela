    Bre Tiesi made a splash in season 6 of Netflix's Selling Sunset. While her real estate know-how was impressive from her very first episode, fans couldn't help but focus on her unique relationship with Nick Cannon. Tiesi and Cannon share a son named Legendary Love Cannon, who is baby number eight for the singer. Since Nick Cannon has 12 children, his personal life is always making headlines.

    Before Bre Tiesi became a reality star famous for being the mother of one of Nick Cannon's children, she was in two other notable relationships. Here's who Tiesi was romantically involved with before she developed feelings for Cannon and became a mom.

    Who Did Selling Sunset Star Bre Tiesi Date Before Nick Cannon?

    Not every new Selling Sunset cast member sticks around, but it seems fair to assume that Bre Tiesi will continue starring in the popular reality series. She commands attention in every scene, knows how to stand up for herself, and has an interesting life.

    Since viewers would hate it if Selling Sunset became boring, Tiesi should definitely be praised for bringing her great personality to the show.

    While Bre Tiesi shared a lot about Nick Cannon on Selling Sunset, the reality series didn't do a deep dive into who she used to date. The reality star has been married once: to Johnny Manziel, a quarterback who played for the NFL. The couple tied the knot in March 2018.

    Around 12 months later, Bre Tiesi and Johnny Manziel were having problems. According to People, Tiesi thought that Johnny had an affair. She talked about it on Instagram and said, “U been married? U been betrayed? I don’t do betrayal for a person I was devoted to period. Vows were broken f— money and f— u and every other loser on here.” However, the post isn't available anymore as it seems that Tiesi got rid of it. It's possible that she didn't want people to message her about what she said.

    A source explained to People that when Tiesi returned home from Bali, she felt that something was wrong. The source said, “She went away, and when she got back, she thought he had been cheating. She somehow found out, and she was irate.”

    RELATED: The Cast Of Selling Sunset, Ranked By Net Worth

    TMZ reported that the divorce was official in November 2021. Since then, it seems that Tiesi is very happy with the way her life is. Page Six reported in January 2023 that she answered some questions on her Instagram account and said that she was "never" interested in being someone's wife. Tiesi said she “only dreamed of traveling the world and being the top of [her] career, creating an empire, creating generational wealth and living life to [the] fullest with” her family."

    She explained that when she and Johnny Manziel tied the knot, she wasn't wearing a wedding dress. She also said they "didn't have a wedding."

    In January 2023, Tiesi said that she loves being a mom to her son and isn't going to add to her family. But in June 2023, she told Entertainment Tonight, “I do and I don't. I think that for where I’m at in my career and my life right now, I just don’t know how I could possibly manage another child.”

    If the allegations are true and Tiesi ended her marriage because of cheating, it makes sense that she wouldn't want to be married again. It also sounds like she is comfortable on her own because, as she has shared on Selling Sunset and on social media, she has many people in her life who help her.

    Bre Tiesi was also in a relationship with Nick Hogan. According to Page Six, they began seeing each other in 2008 and broke up in 2011.

    RELATED: Selling Sunset Stylist Finally Revealed Why The Realtors Wear Over The Top Outfits

    There isn't any information available about Bre and Nick's romance. It doesn't seem that Tiesi has ever talked about being with him. While the romance got some attention since Nick is the son of Hulk Hogan, neither Tiesi nor Nick Hogan has been open about why they broke up.

    However, some publications have talked about Bre Tiesi's physical appearance during the years that she was seeing Nick Hogan. Page Six noted that she has had some plastic surgery since then. Tiesi has mentioned the procedures that she had done. She said on Instagram that she got Botox, filler, and a nose job.

    What Is Selling Sunset Star Bre Tiesi's Relationship With Nick Cannon Like Today?

    In many episodes of season 6 of Selling Sunset, Bre Tiesi mentioned that she and Nick Cannon are still romantically involved, but their romance doesn't look like what people would expect. While other cast members like Chelsea Lazkani questioned it, Tiesi shrugged off the criticism and said that it worked for her.

    According to Today.com, Bre Tiesi talked about Nick Cannon on the Know For Sure podcast in 2022. At the time, she was expecting her baby boy.

    RELATED: The Selling Sunset Season 6 Drama And Feuds That Left Fans Reeling

    Tiesi said that she and the singer don't put labels on what they are. She explained, "It's not necessarily anything. This is just us, this is our relationship and what you do outside of that is what you do outside of that. Everything is respectful, everything is honest. We don't really define it."

    In March 2022, she was interviewed by E! News and called it "a beautiful relationship [where] everything is so supportive and positive."

    During her episodes of Selling Sunset, Bre Tiesi called it an "open relationship." She mentioned that she can see other people and so can Nick.

    In May 2023, Tiesi was interviewed by People and she mentioned how she is portrayed on the reality show. She said, "I think that, yes, there's some drama that is, let's say, dramatized, I'm not really sure the word to use with this one, but there's some of my personal life in a situation that happens with Nick and all of these things that are not fully accurate. So I think that some of that I'm not excited about or thrilled is occurring."

    Tiesi continued to People, "For me, it's just one of those things where people have to take it with a grain of salt and understand that there's a difference. There still is a difference between my real life and the show."
