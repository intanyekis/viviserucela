    Recently, the wrestling veteran appeared at GCW’s C’mon Dude in Hartford. Following that, Justin Credible took to Twitter to share a heartfelt thanks for the wrestling promotion for having him on the show.

    “I want to thank @Lauderdale11 & @GCWrestling_ for having me last night in Hartford. Not only is it great for the fans you make a veteran wrestler feel like he has a place in today’s business. You guys embody the spirit of pro wrestling ?,” Justin Credible tweeted.

    Justin Credible remembers smoking a joint with WWE Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan

    A few weeks ago, Justin Credible was interviewed by Steve Fall of WrestlingNews.co. During the interview, the 50-year-old veteran shared an interesting story about Hulk Hogan. He revealed that following an episode of Monday Night Raw, he and Hulk Hogan smoked a joint together.

    “I remember we were in Minneapolis, I believe, doing TV, and I was with Sean Waltman. We went over to Hulk Hogan’s room because Scott [Hall] and Kevin [Nash] were there. [Hulk] was still in the [in-ring gear] gimmick and we were in his room because we were going to smoke a joint. There I was, smoking a joint with Hulk Hogan after Monday Night RAW. So, that was pretty cool.

    “It was just really mellow. But seeing Hulk Hogan roll it up, I’m like, ‘Yeah…’ [nods his head ‘yes’] And I’m not a [big pot smoker]. I don’t smoke today, not much even then, but seeing him like that, just being cool and chill was interesting. It was cool.”
