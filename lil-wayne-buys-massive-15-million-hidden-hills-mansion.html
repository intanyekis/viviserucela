    Lil Wayne has a lot to celebrate these days. He has his personal life in good order now and his career is progressing with huge success. He has just purchased a massive mansion in the prestigious and highly esteemed area of Hidden Hills. The home is nestled in this very private, gated community, and is said to have been purchased for a whopping $15.4 million.  The massive property spans across 12,135 square feet and includes panoramic views of the stunning surrounding areas. Lil Wayne also happens to be in good company. According to TMZ, his neighbors include Kylie Jenner and the rest of the Kardashian clan.

    RELATED: Check Out Lil' Wayne's New 'Floating' Mansion In Florida

    Living in the lap of luxury is something that Lil Wayne is accustomed to. His musical career has spanned over many years, and his hits continue to generate millions of dollars, and his social media following continues to grow and flourish.He's certainly not new to the fame-game, nor is this his first expensive real estate purchase, but all eyes are on this custom built estate and its bright, open concept living space. Opting to join the Hidden Hills community is a huge step for Lil Wayne, as the property in this region always comes along with a much heftier price tag than most would expect.He'll certainly get what he pays for, as this sprawling space includes more features and amenities than most people would expect to enjoy at a luxury resort. His new home features 7 huge bedrooms and 7.5 bathrooms, each of which is substantially sized. Modern features and decor elements add the perfect finishing touch, and every corner of this home is professionally designed and styled with sheer elegance in mind.

    This gorgeous home includes a huge chef's kitchen which connects to a breakfast room, and that, of course, has it's very own wine display. Lil Wayne now has the perfect place for hosting gatherings, since this mansion includes a stunning entertainer's lounge and its very own custom home theater.

    This spacious abode screams opulence at every turn, and embraces every aspect of a celebrity lifestyle that could ever be imagined. There will be no need for him to ever leave home -he has everything he can ever imagine needing right at home. Whether Lil Wayne is looking for a relaxing getaway, an opportunity to work out, or is hoping to host a party - anything is possible, and there's lots of space to do it in.

    The outdoor pool is expansive, as is the basketball court. There are a series of patios that each have their own seating areas, as well as a guest house situated on the property that features a stunning 1 bedroom living space.
